# CEO-OKRs-2026 (Apex)

**Timeframe:** Calendar year 2026  
**Owner:** CEO (this channel)  
**Operating principle:** Apex builds infrastructure for long-term athlete development — not feature noise.

---

## Objective 1 — Prove club-first PMF in basketball
**Thesis:** If we can’t reliably acquire + retain basketball clubs, nothing else matters.

**Key Results (by Dec 31, 2026)**
1. **120 net-new paying clubs** (logos) on standard packaging (no bespoke).
2. **Activation ≥ 70%** of new clubs within 30 days, defined as:
   - ≥ 3 teams created, and
   - schedule published for the next 2 weeks, and
   - ≥ 1 practice plan created.
3. **Logo retention ≥ 85% at 12 months** for clubs that onboard in 2026.
4. **Engagement:** Median **≥ 6 weekly active teams per active club**.

---

## Objective 2 — Become operationally indispensable to club directors (switching costs)
**Thesis:** We win when a director can’t run the club week-to-week without Apex.

**Key Results (by Dec 31, 2026)**
1. **Director workflow adoption:** ≥ **60%** of active clubs have a director/admin who is **weekly active** in Apex.
2. **Admin time savings:** demonstrate **≥ 30% reduction** in “time to run the week” for directors/admins (survey + workflow completion proxies).
3. **Director NPS ≥ 40** (measured separately from coaches/parents).

---

## Objective 3 — Sustainable revenue economics (capital efficiency)
**Thesis:** Growth that can’t be profitably scaled is a trap.

**Key Results (by Dec 31, 2026)**
1. **ARPA ≥ $3,000 / club / year** (blended), driven by tiering/packaging.
2. **Gross margin ≥ 75%** (including infra + AI costs).
3. **Net revenue retention (NRR) ≥ 115%** via expansion within clubs (more teams, higher tiers), not custom work.

---

## Objective 4 — Reliability + product discipline (trust is the moat)
**Thesis:** Clubs pay for certainty. Reliability beats novelty.

**Key Results (by Dec 31, 2026)**
1. **99.9% uptime** for core workflows (auth, scheduling, comms, practice plans).
2. **P1 MTTR < 60 minutes**.
3. **Onboarding funnel discipline:** ≥ **80%** of new clubs complete the onboarding path (invite → roster → schedule → first week run) within 30 days.

---

## Objective 5 — AI that measurably improves athlete development (no hype)
**Thesis:** AI is only defensible if it changes outcomes and remains cost-controlled.

**Key Results (by Dec 31, 2026)**
1. Ship **2 AI-assisted coaching workflows** with **≥ 40% adoption** among weekly active coaches at active clubs.
2. Demonstrate **≥ 10% improvement** in a leading indicator tied to athlete development at clubs using those workflows (metric to be chosen and instrumented; examples: practice-plan completion, attendance consistency, skill progression logging completeness).
3. **AI cost cap:** ≤ **$1.00 per active team per month** (blended) while meeting performance targets.

---

## CEO Decision Commitments (how we’ll stay accountable)
- We will **reject** initiatives that do not tie to club acquisition, retention, expansion revenue, or measurable athlete development.
- We will ship **fewer**, higher-leverage workflows and hold reliability + onboarding as non-negotiable.
- Any AI feature must include: (1) adoption target, (2) measurable outcome metric, (3) explicit unit-cost cap.
