# COO-Risk-Register (Apex)

> Living list of risks; reviewed weekly.

| Risk | Type | Severity | Leading indicator | Mitigation | Owner |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Building before selling (no real club usage) | Market | High | <10 director calls/week; no design partners | CEO-led outreach; design partner program; ship only workflows demanded by directors | CEO |
| Scope sprawl / bespoke club requests | Product/Operational | High | Custom asks enter backlog | “No bespoke” policy; every item must map to a KR; Two-option rule | COO |
| Weak activation (clubs don’t reach first-week value) | Product | High | <70% onboarding completion | Instrument funnel; simplify onboarding; CS playbook; weekly iteration | COO |
| Reliability issues undermine trust | Product/Brand | Medium | P1s, downtime, high support volume | Weekly release train + incident discipline; prioritize core workflow stability | Eng pod |
| Pricing/packaging unclear → low ARPA | Financial | Medium | Discounts, ad hoc pricing | 3-tier packaging; standard contracts; expansion motion | CEO/COO |
| AI cost creep | Financial/Operational | Medium | AI $/active team rising | Cost cap; measure unit costs; prefer deterministic automation when possible | COO |
